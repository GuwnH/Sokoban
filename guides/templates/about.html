About SokoGuides

What is Sokoban?

Sokoban (倉庫番, meaning "warehouse keeper") is a classic puzzle game where players:

  • Navigate a character through a maze-like warehouse
  • Push boxes/crates to designated storage locations
  • Use only cardinal directions (up, down, left, right)
  • Plan carefully - boxes can't be pulled, only pushed
  • Solve increasingly complex spatial puzzles

First created in 1982, Sokoban has inspired countless variants and remains popular today for its simple mechanics but challenging puzzles.
