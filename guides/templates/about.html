What is Sokoban?

Sokoban (倉庫番, meaning "warehouse keeper") is a classic puzzle game where players:

  • Navigate a warehouse worker through maze-like levels
  • Push boxes to marked storage locations
  • Can only move in four directions (up, down, left, right)
  • Must plan carefully - boxes can't be pulled, only pushed
  • Solve increasingly complex spatial puzzles
Key Features:
Simple controls but deep puzzles
Over 300 original levels in classic version
Develops logical thinking and planning skills
